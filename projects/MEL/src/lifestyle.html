A Thriving Community in the Heart of East Peoria

Nesteled close the head of Springiel Road, Mel Hasty Retirement Center ovelooks bustling Main Street in the heart of downtown East Peoria. The recipent of the East Peoria Beautification award stands fives stories tall and looks better than it ever has in its 30-year-history thanks to a $3.5 million dollar rehab taht started in 2015.

Fresh exterior paint, new windows, and a brand new roof were high priorities, but the senior living community spared no expense to update is look. The grounds are now dotted with picnic benches, a honey patio, and lush new landscaping. The interior received a full overhaul: each of the 60 units received a remodel and update, including a new facility-wide sprinkler system, two emergency pull cords in every apartment, and individual heat and AC controls. Even the in-house library and the common areas received some love: stepping into the freshly carpted lobby isn't like entering a medical facility or hotel lobby; it is like coming home.
