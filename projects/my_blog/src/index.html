Love is a choice

Have you been going through a difficult time with marriage, girlfriend, bodyfriend, or whatever? Well, I know just where you're coming from. I never understood the difficulties of a relationship. When I first got married six years ago, I was still happy and had all kinds of funny feelings for my wife. But, those feelings, well they soon dissapate and when they do its hard to overcome hardships in a relationship.

Well, this is what I have been experincing for about the last two-three years, maybe? I want to share my story with Dad's who have been seperated from their spouse. Not only the spouse, but sometimes there are children involved... Unfortunately, My wife and I have a four year old son. I moved out two months ago and being away from your child is the hardest thing I've ever done in my life. I get to see him once a week and every other weekend so it's not too bad, but still I feel like a "weekend Dad", or someting.

Well, enough blabbing if you look below I posted some pictures of my family just so you get a feel for who I am I guess. Don't forget to read the blog as well. Over on my blog you can leave comments if you'd like after you're done reading and I'll be sure to respond. Thank you and have a blessed day/night, wherever you are

-Justin A. Maynard, Love is not just a feeling it's a choice
